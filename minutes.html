Open Door Toastmasters Meeting Minutes

Word of the Day :Quixotic

Speeches:

Ashish Rajbar: Leadership Development – Project 3: Researching and presenting (5-7 mins) – Happiness

Ashish explained how happiness comes from within. We need to focus on helping each other, volunteering and noticing all the good that surrounds us. Ultimately, it is our choice to face each day with gratitude; which is the key to a happy life.

Bhumika Rajbar: Innovative Planning – Project 2: Evaluation and Feedback (5-7 mins) – Mobile Technology: Boon or Bane

The purpose of this project is for the member to demonstrate that he or she has applied feedback received from his or her first speech. Smartphones assist in emergencies, employment and dating to name a few, but can be distracting and cause us to lose concentration if we allow it to. We can become addicted to its use and rely on it for everything. The bottom line is that smartphones are getting smarter and people are getting dumber.

Willie Markert, ACB, CL: Motivation – Icebreaker (4-6 minutes) – A Life Changing Event

Willie gave us a wonderful account of his time in the military service. He then went on to pursue a career in finance. Thank you for your service, Willie!
